Export Chat Transcript From LiveEngage

  1. Log into your LiveEngage account. On the visitors tab, click the dropdown to show the reporting dashboard.


  2. Click on the 'export chat' icon on the left side of the screen.


  3. Choose your timeframe, click 'additional filters' and make sure to select 'XML'.
