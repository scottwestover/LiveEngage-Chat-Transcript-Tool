How To Find Your Order Total Variable

  1. Open your chat transcript XML file in a program that will format the XML, for example Chrome.


  2. Scroll to the very bottom of the file. At the bottom you will see a section called 'Variables'.


  3. Look for a variable where the title is your question that is on the agent survey. Your Order ID variable will be the id of that variable.
